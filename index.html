One of the most important steps in becoming a computer programmer is making sure it is right for you! Research will help you determine if you are on the right course to meet your goals! Preparedness is crucial in the path to discovering if you will be ready and willing to undertake the journey required to become a successful programmer. The goal of this website is to help assist you in your decision by suggesting a career path and/or language track that you would be both interested and successful at. While the suggestions may not be perfect, they can help to narrow your own search results and open the door to further research.
